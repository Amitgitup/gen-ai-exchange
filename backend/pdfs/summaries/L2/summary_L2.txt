The Jharkhand government announced several new industrial development policies aimed at boosting the state's economy.  These policies focus on food processing, electric vehicles, ethanol, MSMEs, and exports, offering fiscal incentives (subsidies, tax breaks) and non-fiscal support (infrastructure, skill development).  Specific targets for investment, job creation, and market share are included.  The policies prioritize sustainable and inclusive growth, supporting marginalized groups, and improving governance through streamlined processes and online platforms.  Detailed incentive schemes, eligibility criteria, and implementation procedures are provided for each policy.
